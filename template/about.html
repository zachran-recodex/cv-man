Nusantara Mould

Nusantara Mould is an innovative product designed to support micro, small, and medium enterprises (SMEs) especially traditional producers of soybean by offering a cost-efficient and practical solution for the production process. This product emphasizes flexibility and affordability, helping small-scale tempeh sellers streamline their operations without the need for expensive, industrial-scale equipment. What sets Nusantara Mould apart is its multi-functional design, combining several essential tools into one compact and user-friendly unit. By integrating various production steps into a single product, users can save space, reduce production time, and lower operational costs.
